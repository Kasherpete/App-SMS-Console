About the service

 The App SMS Service is a revolutionary solution that aims to bridge the gap between the technology divide by offering a simple and accessible means of communication for individuals who lack access to an app store or the internet. With this service, users can conveniently carry out a variety of functions, such as sending a variety of commands, checking the weather forecast, and accessing basic information, all through text messaging.

 The Eth0s group and its partners are committed to providing a seamless experience for all users of this service. They have put in place robust measures to ensure the reliability, security, and efficiency of the system, which are being constantly updated all the time.  By leveraging the power of text messaging, the App SMS Service has made it possible for people from all walks of life to access critical services and information without any hassle.
